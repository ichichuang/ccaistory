## Narrative Function / 叙事功能

Commit to the shortcut; the lamps thin out and the light gaps widen. Unease rises. Plants the first clue — thinning lamps mean they are heading the wrong way (paid off later as "wandered into the open country").

## Canonical Scene Description / 规范场景描述

The shortcut lane. A few street lamps stand ever farther apart, their warm pools of light shrinking with growing gaps of dark between them. A faint low mist begins to gather; trees draw closer along the verge.
