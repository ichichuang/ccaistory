# 黄昏岔路口 / The Fork at Dusk

> Canonical Scene card — pilot-001 (Cluster 3). Pages: p1. Child-safe. Execution packages are downstream (blocked).

## Narrative Function / 叙事功能

Opening hook + setup. Establishes the choice: the long familiar road home vs. an inviting unknown shortcut, as night begins to fall. Plants curiosity and the first hint of unease.

## Canonical Scene Description / 规范场景描述

Dusk. Xiao He and Mama stand at a fork in the road. One way is bright and familiar; the other is a shadowy lane curving into trees. A wooden signpost stands between them. The sky is deep gold fading to blue, with the first star or two.

## Required Characters / 所需角色

- `../30-characters/char-xiaohe.md`
- `../30-characters/char-mama.md`

## Required Props / 所需道具

Wooden signpost; the two diverging roads (bright vs. shadowy); dusk sky.

## Visual Composition Notes / 构图

Wide establishing shot; the fork centers the frame; warm light on the familiar road, cool shadow on the shortcut, to externalize the choice. Child and caregiver small against the landscape.

## Continuity Constraints / 连续性约束

Xiao He in yellow raincoat + red boots; Mama in teal coat. Dusk lighting here is the brightest of the journey; light then darkens through the next two scenes.
