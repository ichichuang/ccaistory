# 黑黑的林间小路 / The Dark Wooded Lane

> Canonical Scene card — pilot-001 (Cluster 3). Pages: p3–p6 (the three gentle "scares" + the calming turn). Child-safe. Execution packages downstream (blocked).

## Narrative Function / 叙事功能

Fear peak, then the turn. Holds the three scary-seeming things — two glowing dots, a low moaning sound, and a pale drifting shape — each of which is later given an ordinary explanation. At p6 the story turns: Mama and Xiao He stop, hold hands, breathe, and choose to stay calm and find help.

## Canonical Scene Description / 规范场景描述

Full dark. Tall trees lean close over the lane; it feels cold, with breath-fog and thin mist. Ahead, two soft dots glow faintly (Tangerine's eyes, not yet revealed). A low moan drifts on the air (wind in a hollow fence-pipe). A pale shape slips low across the lane (mist). At p6, the two figures pause close together, take a steadying breath — a small pocket of calm.

## Visual Composition Notes / 构图

Tighter, darker frames; the glowing dots small and ambiguous. Keep all imagery "spooky-cute," never genuinely horrific. The p6 turn gets a calmer composition — the two figures close and centered, a steadying beat that begins to release tension.

## Continuity Constraints / 连续性约束

The dots, the fence-pipe, and the mist must be placed and shaped so their cottage-side payoffs (cat eyes, wind, mist) are visually consistent. Fear stays gentle throughout.
