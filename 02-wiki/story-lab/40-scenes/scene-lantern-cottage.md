# 守灯小屋 / The Lantern Cottage

> Canonical Scene card — pilot-001 (Cluster 3). Pages: p7–p11 (rescue + all three ordinary-cause payoffs). Child-safe. Execution packages downstream (blocked).

## Narrative Function / 叙事功能

Rescue and reveal. The warm cottage is the answer to "ask for help." Lantern Grandpa welcomes them and gently shows the ordinary cause behind each scary-seeming thing: the glowing eyes were his cat; the moan was wind in a hollow fence-pipe; the pale shape was drifting mist; and they only felt so lost and cold because they had wandered off the lit road into the open fields.

## Canonical Scene Description / 规范场景描述

A cozy cottage with a glowing window and an open, welcoming door. Lantern Grandpa stands with his lantern; Tangerine the cat appears, eyes catching the lantern light (the "glow"). Just outside, a hollow fence-pipe the wind sings through; low mist curls over open fields beyond. Warm light pools against the dark.

## Visual Composition Notes / 构图

Warm pool of light versus the dark behind. Faces lit gently and kindly. Stage each payoff so it visually rhymes with its earlier scare (dots ↔ cat eyes; sound ↔ fence-pipe; pale shape ↔ mist). Warmest light of the whole journey.

## Continuity Constraints / 连续性约束

Tangerine = orange tabby, white paws, bell collar; Lantern Grandpa with lantern + round glasses + brown cardigan. The fence-pipe and mist must match the dark-lane scene so the reveals land. Grandpa always reads as safe and kind.
