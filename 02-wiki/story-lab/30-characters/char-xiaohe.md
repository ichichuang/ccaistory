# 小禾 / Xiao He

> Canonical Character card — identity + appearance lock for the accepted pilot-001 story core (Cluster 3). Rendering style (`visual_style`) is assigned in Workflow D and accepted by the Workflow D human gate. Original and child-safe; no source-derived identity.

## Role in Story / 故事中的定位

Protagonist. A curious, kind child (~6–7) traveling home at dusk with their caregiver. Takes the unfamiliar shortcut, feels the fear of the dark, and — with help — learns that scary-seeming things have ordinary explanations and that asking for help is brave. Carries the emotional arc: curiosity → fear → choosing calm → relief → pride.

## Canonical Description / 规范外观描述

A young child about six or seven, with a round, friendly face and bright, curious eyes. Short dark hair held with a small clip. Wears a buttercup-yellow hooded raincoat (it drizzles on the journey), red rubber boots, and a small round backpack. Warm, simple, rounded shapes.

## Visual Constraints / 视觉锁定

- Child proportions (~6–7), never older or younger.
- Yellow hooded raincoat + red boots across all journey pages.
- Short dark hair with a small clip; small round backpack.

## Expression and Pose Notes / 表情与动作

Allowed: curious, gently scared (wide eyes, a small clutch of the caregiver's hand), a steadying breath, relieved smile, proud, cozy/sleepy. Not allowed: terror, hysterical crying, anguish — fear stays gentle and safe.

## Scene Usage / 场景使用

Appears in every scene (p1–p14). See `../40-scenes/`.

## Forbidden Changes / 禁止变化

- No aging up/down; no swapping the yellow raincoat / red boots.
- No horror or realistic styling; fear never depicted as traumatic.
