# 橘子 / Tangerine the Cat

> Canonical Character card — pilot-001 (Cluster 3). `visual_style` assigned in Workflow D and accepted by the Workflow D human gate. Original and child-safe.

## Role in Story / 故事中的定位

Lantern Grandpa's friendly cat — and the ordinary explanation for the "glowing eyes" that frightened Xiao He on the dark lane. Provides the warm, giggly payoff that turns fear into relief.

## Canonical Description / 规范外观描述

A plump, friendly orange tabby with white paws and a small bell collar. Big round eyes that catch and reflect the lantern's light (the "glow").

## Visual Constraints / 视觉锁定

- Orange tabby; white paws; small bell collar; big round reflective eyes.

## Expression and Pose Notes / 表情与动作

Allowed: curious, friendly, purring, playful. Not allowed: menacing, hostile, or eyes glowing as if by magic.

## Scene Usage / 场景使用

Appears p9 (and may cameo p10–p11 at the cottage). See `../40-scenes/`.

## Forbidden Changes / 禁止变化

- Always cute and friendly; keep markings + bell collar; the eye-glow is reflection only.
